Trivia

  • Damien's parents are members of the High Court of Hell, thus they have been training him to be a future member since he was born.
  • He dislikes the idea of being on the High Court, and has always wanted to be an explorer instead, traveling Hell and even Earth to learn about them up close. Hell, specifically, is massive and has many unexplored areas to this day as people feared the unknown that lay there.
  • Damien's humanly form was stolen from a man in Earth realm whom he witnessed in his unauthorized travels. Demons have the ability to mask themselves in any way they please. Though for Damien, despite being stolen, he is most comfortable in his form and considers it the "real him." Similarly, demons are all genderless beings, but Damien likes to be referred to as male.
  • Damien and Jonah's souls are linked to one another's, naturally drawing them to each other. The two end up getting married in Hell in the future.
  • He may seem scary and threatening, but he's incredibly gentle and even naive at times. He admires all the cute merchandise when he goes to Earth stores.
  • There are multiple subspecies of demon, and Damien is considered an Elemental Demon. Even more specifically, a Magma Demon.
  • Even Damien isn't quite sure what his markings mean if anything, but his parents have them as well.
  • Despite preferring things on the peaceful side, he has fun adventuring with Jonah and Celia and sending Hell beasts to whence they came.
  • He has very little combat training, but he is quite powerful. He possesses magma powers and fights with a pitchfork.
  • Due to Damien not being from Earth, he comes across as strange to other humans especially when he's trying to blend in with them such as at school. For example, he is both too polite but also too blunt, giving him a tendency to be too honest with others but believing he's being polite and respectful in doing so.
