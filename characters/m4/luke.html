Personality

Luke is a rather quiet and reserved person who, like his muse abilities, can have a fiery side when provoked. He tries his best to keep his mellow composure, but deep down is quite easy to anger. Winky likes to theorize that this was specifically caused by his muse abilities, as he wasn't quite like this beforehand. He's is a bit gullible, sometimes confusing jokes for reality, and getting incredibly embarrassed after making these confusions. Luke has trouble making and keeping friends which has really decreased his confidence. He's been abandoned quite frequently, though he's very unsure as to why. Thus, he struggles to trust other people or rely on them. He becomes very desperate and frenzied when he gets the feeling people will leave him again, causing him to do very reckless things even if it means people will get hurt. But he's since learned that all the people that had left him never truly cared about him, and it's the people like Winky who stay who are his real friends. Nowadays, while he can never quite get over his fears, he has made better friends that he tries his best to open up to. He's very protective and always willing to fight for them. Luke is an avid drummer, loving to play ever since he was little. Practicing would be difficult due to his parents' disappointment in this choice of hobby, but he was able to learn and become quite talented through sheer determination. He now plays for his own band that's rising in popularity. As of the band's revival, he was encouraged to learn vocals as well due to being the band's leader and lyricist, now delivering powerful vocals that well-accompany his captivating writing. Due to his harsh upbringing, he speaks in a sort of polite manner that's difficult to shake. Thus, he's viewed as a 'doormat' by others, and usually on the receiving end of antics. He's not one to speak up or defend himself when he's being bullied or discomforted, and just takes the hits as they come. Completely contrary to how he stands up for others. Despite living by himself, Luke isn't the best at taking care of himself, usually being caught up in his interests or busy life. He's known to put others way before himself as well. Not because he particularly wants to neglect himself, it's more so just a habit.
