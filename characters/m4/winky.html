Personality

Winky is a very eccentric individual, often saying or doing bizarre things. He simultaneously has a crude and silly lighthearted sense of humor with little filter or room awareness. He's very hyperactive, always having boundless energy to zip around and talk endlessly (and loudly) at any hour of the day. All around, he does try to be friendly with everyone he meets, although many aren't willing to return the favor, seeing him as strange. He hates being hurtful towards others and starting conflict even if they mistreat him, being overly tolerant of any such thing that comes his way. He has only been known to snap in very specific circumstance, in which he can get quite emotional and reckless against his better judgement. All of which have been over his partner Luke, whom he spent his childhood being incredibly possessive of, warding off as many potential suitors as possible. Despite Winky's demeanor, he's very determined and adamant about being seen as cute by others, and gets very upset when he's (very often) told otherwise by everyone but Luke. Winky is very attached to bunnies to the point of identifying himself with them as well, which contributes a lot to his need to be cute. He likes to call himself an "alien bunny DJ from outer space," though no one can quite gauge if this is a genuine belief or Winky playing around as he does. Winky is relatively carefree, just doing whatever he pleases and not letting anything tie him down. He has an almost concerning lack of fear or shame in most situations, being able to do things like striking crazy conversations with strangers. He's also very much a people pleaser, going hand in hand with his hatred of conflict. He tries his hardest to get everyone to like him and make them happy, even at his own detriment. Winky is a very kind person, always ensuring everyone's wellbeing and happiness. He absolutely loves spoiling his favorite people. Even if he's always loud and hyper, he can be down to Earth and a good listener when he needs to be, doing his best to advise those around him and work out their problems. Winky is blind, but has been given "vision" through his headset. It records his surroundings then projects it into his head like a daydream. He has had severe degenerative blindness since infancy, currently no longer having vision at all. He doesn't seem to mind his lack of vision due to loving his headset so much and almost always living this way, only being bothered when he gives it too much thought.
